A Short Bio

Maieuran is a dynamic dancer with 15 years of experience, specializing in breaking and proficient in a wide range of styles, including bollywood, contemporary hip hop, waacking, popping and the classical Indian dance style Odissi. Known for blending diverse influences, Maieuran brings a unique energy and storytelling to every performance. Driven by creativity and a passion for movement, Maieuran continues to push the boundaries of dance, creating innovative work that inspires and connects with audiences.
